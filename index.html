Resist the hords and conquer the world!

Conqueror's Crown: New Horizon is an awesome RTS video game in which you are lockdown in a place where every now and then a horde of orcs will attack you. Make a good strategy and stay alive as much as possible!

Fortify your base and survive the hords...

Build walls to protect your base, put your soldiers inside and resist the orc waves.

..or prepare your army to destroy their bases!

Create a powerful army and go all over the map to destroy enemy bases to reach the glory.

Collect resources and explore the whole map

Make your gatherers work tirelessly to take resources such as gold, stone or wood. But watch out! They are limited, so go throught the map to find more mines, forests and quarrys.

Controls

In-Game && tutorial:

Left click mouse to select units, buildings or click into buttons, and approve a construction.
Right click mouse to move selected units, select an enemy to attack or deny constructions.
W,A,S,D : Camera movement (also with left click into the minimap).
X: skip tutorial (only tutorial).
F10: God mode (in this mode the player doesn't use resources when creating troops/buldings/level ups, and friendly troops don't receive damage). While F10 active:
F1: Gain 100 of Wood,Stone and Gold (Resources).
F2: Skip to defeat scene (only in-game).
F3: Skip to victory scene (only in-game).
F5: Save current game state (only in-game).
F6: Load previous game state.
1: Spawn Swordman.
2: Spawn Archer.
3: Spawn FootMan.
4: Spawn Knight.
5: Spawn Troll.
6: Spawn Ogre.
7: Spawn Orc.
Q: Build Townhall.
E: Build Barracks.
R: Build Laboratory.
T: Build Walls.
Q: Build Stables.
